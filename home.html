OUR STORY

Tru-Cape is a prominent fruit production and marketing company located in the Ceres Valley, Western Cape, South Africa. It was established in 1991, the company has grown to become one of the leading suppliers of quality fresh fruit in the country.

We believe in the power of community and strive to make a positive impact through our quality fruits. We are dedicated to offering quality assurance, sustainability, transparency, taste and freshness including unique varieties when it comes to our products.
