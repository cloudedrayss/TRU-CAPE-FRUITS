Our Journey

Established in 1991, It was formed through a merger of various growers in the Ceres and surrounding regions, allowing for a more efficient distribution model and a unified approach to marketing their fruit.

They cater for individuals, families, caregivers, those in the food service industry, eco-conscious consumers and retailers who are seeking for fresh, nutritious, healthy, and high-value quality fruits.

Our Values

We are known for our commitment to producing high-quality fruit, particularly apples and pears, as well as our dedication to Sustainability and community involvement. Only superior produce which meets our exacting standards is selected. Our values include:

  • Quality Ingredients: We only supply our freshest fruits to all stores, sourced locally whenever possible.
  • Community: We are proud to be part of the Witpoortjie community and give back through various charitable initiatives.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to sustainable practices, from packaging to picking the freshest fruits.
